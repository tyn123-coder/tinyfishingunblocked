What is King Fishing?

King Fishing is an arcade game developed by IGS (International Games System) in 2006. Players control a shooting gun and reel to catch virtual fish appearing on the screen. With realistic 2D graphics and lively sound effects, King Fishing provides players with the sensation of real fishing.

    • Basic Information about King Fishing

  • Graphics: Realistic 2D graphics create a lively fishing experience.
  • Fish Variety: The game features a total of 28 different fish species, ranging from small fish to giant ones.
  • Levels: There are a total of 8 levels.
  • Difficulty Levels: The game offers 4 different difficulty levels, from easy to hard, suitable for players of all skill levels.
  • Rewards: Players can earn rewards in the form of coins after completing each level or catching special fish.
  • Scores: Players can review their scores and compare them with other players on the leaderboard.

    • Playing Experience of King Fishing

  • King Fishing is not just a simple entertainment game; it also provides players with exciting and engaging experiences. With realistic 2D graphics, you will feel like you are actually fishing in a natural environment. Vibrant sound effects along with vibrating sensations when the fish bite also create a realistic and captivating atmosphere.
  • Moreover, the variety of fish species and different levels make the game more diverse and interesting. You will need to experiment and find ways to catch each type of fish to complete each level. This makes the game more challenging and requires players to have their own skills and strategies to catch the big fish.

    • Things to Note and Effective Gameplay Tips

  • Choose the appropriate level: Before starting, review the levels and choose one that suits your abilities. Don't pick a level that's too difficult if you're just starting out.
  • Wait for the right moment: Sometimes, waiting is an effective strategy. Try shooting a few times before starting to catch bigger fish.
  • Track the leaderboard: Keep an eye on the leaderboard to see your score and compare it with other players. This will also motivate you to try to complete each level.
