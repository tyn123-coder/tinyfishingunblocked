GOLFINITY - Unlimited Golf Game for All Gamers

GOLFINITY is one of the games developed by Tiny Fishing, an independent game company based in the United States. It's a simple and fun mini golf game where you can challenge your skills and patience in getting the ball into the hole. With increasingly difficult levels and minimalist yet colorful graphics, GOLFINITY promises to provide you with enjoyable moments of entertainment.

True to its name - The Endless Mini Golf Game

With its intriguing name, GOLFINITY has truly lived up to players' expectations. Unlike traditional golf games, in GOLFINITY, you don't have to wait for your turn or calculate the height of your shot. You can play continuously without waiting for your turn or your opponent's turn. This is indeed an "endless" mini golf game, suitable for sports game enthusiasts who don't have much time to spare.

Your Goal - Get the Ball into the Hole in a Certain Number of Moves

In GOLFINITY, your goal is to get the ball into the hole within a certain number of moves. Each level will have a maximum number of moves allowed, and you must find a way to get the ball into the hole within that limit. If you exceed the allowed number of moves, you'll have to replay the level. However, not all levels are equally easy, as you progress and unlock new levels, more complex designs will appear, making it harder to get the ball into the hole.

The initial levels are quite simple, but the game gets more interesting as you play

As you start playing, you'll encounter the initial levels with simple and easier designs. But don't be too quick to rejoice, because the subsequent levels will require you to be more skillful and control the ball more accurately. You'll encounter sloping paths, jumps, areas without side walls, loops, and various other obstacles to test your skills and patience.

Prepare yourself for new challenges and continuously improve your skills as you progress through each level in GOLFINITY. You'll need to use all your abilities to get the ball into the hole and unlock new levels.

Minimalist Graphics, yet Exciting

One of the special features of GOLFINITY is its minimalist yet exciting graphics. The combination of bright colors and simple graphics creates a colorful and vibrant gaming environment. You'll enjoy beautiful landscapes and unique designs in each level.

The simple graphics are also a plus for this game because you can play smoothly without being interrupted by complex graphic effects. This also allows the game to run on various devices without errors or delays.

Simple and Easy-to-Understand Gameplay

The gameplay in GOLFINITY is extremely simple and easy to understand. Just tap anywhere on the screen and drag your finger in the direction you want to hit the ball. You can adjust the shot by changing the width and length of the control bar. This control bar will help you determine the direction and force of the shot, making it easier to play.

Another advantage of this gameplay is that you can play from any position on the screen, so you're not limited by the size or orientation of the screen. This will help you focus more on getting the ball into the hole without worrying about controlling the ball.

Conclusion

In conclusion, GOLFINITY is a simple yet extremely fun and addictive mini golf game. With minimalist yet colorful graphics and easy-to-understand gameplay, this game is truly a great choice for sports game enthusiasts. Try your hand at GOLFINITY and conquer the challenging levels to become a skilled golfer!
