Welcome to FNAF3

FNAF3 continues the chilling saga of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, plunging you into another nightmarish adventure filled with terror and suspense. In this installment, you'll return to the abandoned pizzeria as a new night security guard, tasked with surviving five harrowing nights while evading the vengeful spirits of the past.

The dilapidated pizzeria is now a haunted attraction, but the malevolent spirits of the animatronics still linger, seeking revenge on those who wronged them. Armed with only your wits and a handful of tools, you must navigate the eerie corridors and fend off the vengeful spirits that lurk within.

Unlike previous entries, FNAF3 introduces a new gameplay mechanic: the use of audio devices to lure and distract the animatronics. Strategically deploy audio cues to lead the spirits away from your location, buying precious time to uncover the dark secrets hidden within the pizzeria.

As the nights progress, the horrors become increasingly intense, with new challenges and obstacles testing your resolve at every turn. Can you survive the horrors of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza once again, or will you become another victim of its cursed legacy?

Key Features:

  • A terrifying new chapter in the FNAF saga, set in the haunted halls of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza.
  • Unique gameplay mechanics, including the use of audio devices to distract and evade the animatronics.
  • Immersive atmosphere filled with suspenseful moments, jump scares, and hidden lore waiting to be uncovered.
  • Multiple nights of escalating difficulty, each with its own unique challenges and surprises.
  • An engaging storyline that delves deeper into the dark history of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza and its cursed animatronics.

Prepare yourself for another night of terror in FNAF3. Can you survive the horrors that await, or will you succumb to the darkness?
