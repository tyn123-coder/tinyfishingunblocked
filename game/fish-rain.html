    4. Fishing Techniques in Fish Rain

    Fly Fishing:

    Fly fishing is a popular technique used in Fish Rain, especially for catching trout and salmon. It involves using a lightweight lure, known as a fly, to mimic the movement of insects on the water's surface. This technique requires precision and patience, making it a favorite among experienced anglers.

    Bait Fishing:

    Bait fishing is the most common and straightforward technique used in Fish Rain. It involves using bait, such as worms or fish eggs, to attract fish to your hook. This technique is suitable for catching a wide variety of fish species and is perfect for beginners.

    Trolling:

    Trolling is a technique used for catching larger fish species, such as tuna and marlin. It involves dragging a lure behind a moving boat, mimicking the movement of a swimming fish. This technique requires a bit of skill and timing, but the reward is worth it when you catch a massive fish.
