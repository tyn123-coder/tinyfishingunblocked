    • Parkour Race

    Parkour Race is a 3D running game developed by Madbox. Take control of your stickman and race against the crowd. You can run, dash or jump from rooftop to rooftop! Run over the glowing speed bumpers to gain momentum and do vaults and flips to show off your style. There are daily challenges, power-ups, and even glamorous accessories for you to create a stickman that looks like no other. Take your running game to next level with Parkour Race!

    How to play:

  • Move Left - A or Left arrow key
  • Move Right - D or Right arrow key

    • About the creator:

    Parkour Race was created by Madbox, a French game development studio known for its addictive and innovative mobile games. Founded in 2018, Madbox has quickly become one of the leading game developers in the industry, with millions of downloads and numerous awards under their belt. With their expertise in creating casual and hyper-casual games, Madbox has once again delivered a hit with Parkour Race.

    ParkourRace

    One of the most exciting aspects of Parkour Race is its endless levels. As you progress in the game, the challenges become tougher, and the obstacles become more complex. The ParkourRace destination is where the real test begins. This level features a never-ending course filled with obstacles, jumps, and slides, making it an ideal destination for parkour enthusiasts.

    Obstacles

    The ParkourRace level is full of challenging obstacles that will test your reflexes and agility. From fire rings and swinging axes to giant hammers and moving platforms, this level will keep you on your toes and require quick decision-making skills. But don't worry, the more you play, the better you will become at overcoming these obstacles.

    Power-ups

    To help you navigate through the obstacles, Parkour Race offers various power-ups that give you temporary boosts. These include a shield that protects you from obstacles, a magnet that attracts coins towards you, and a time-slowing power-up that gives you more time to plan your moves. Keep an eye out for these power-ups as they can make a significant impact on your race!

    Coins

    As you progress in the ParkourRace level, you will come across coins scattered throughout the course. Collecting these coins will earn you points, which you can use to unlock new stickman characters and accessories. The more coins you collect, the higher your score will be, so make sure to grab as many as you can!
