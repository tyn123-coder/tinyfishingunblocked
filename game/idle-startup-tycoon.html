Idle startup tycoon

Run an innovative business and grow your company in the simulation game Idle Startup Tycoon. Start your entrepreneurial adventure with a food delivery company, hire employees, managers and generate your first profits. Reinvest the money earned to create new businesses with higher added value and improve existing ones to increase their profitability. So your small business will become a large multinational company with many floors in a building. The improvements available will be almost unlimited, if your business manages to grow enough investors will contact you to offer you even more challenges!
