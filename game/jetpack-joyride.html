JetPack JoyRide

Take control of Barry the Flying Man and try to travel the longest distance possible in the hit arcade game JetPack Joyride. Fly around the laboratory with your JetPack, avoid electric traps and missiles, shoot scientists on the ground and collect as many gold as possible on each attempt. Sometimes bonuses will appear and give you access to two special vehicles, the Lil Stomper robot and the Badass Bike motorcycle, which will allow you to progress more easily but also to get hit by a trap once without dying. Try to stay focused as much as possible to get as far as possible in the lab and beat your previous records. You can play this free online version of JetPck Joyride without download and on all devices, computer, tablet and mobile.
