Crazy Fishing

Crazy Fishing: A Simple yet Addictive Adventure

Have you ever dreamed of standing by the seashore, fishing, and feeling the salty breeze of the ocean? Crazy Fishing will take you to the colorful world of fishing, where you can challenge yourself and hunt for unique fish species.

Game Information:

  • Genre: Fishing
  • Graphics: 2D
  • Platform: Web
  • Release Date: 2022
  • Price: Free

    • Genre and Gameplay:

    Crazy Fishing belongs to the fishing game genre and features simple gameplay. You just need to use the mouse cursor to catch fish. Different types of fish appear continuously, and you must seize the opportunity to earn money.

    Earning Money:

    An interesting aspect is that you have to spend quite some time to accumulate the first $1000 to upgrade equipment. This creates a challenge and continuous exploration in the game.

    Types of Fish:

    In Crazy Fishing, you'll encounter various types of fish such as pufferfish, sharks, clownfish, and more. Each fish corresponds to a monetary value ranging from $1 to $100. Hunt them down to earn as much money as possible!

    Notable Challenges:

    Although there aren't many challenges, the game is still addictive. You can play it in your free time, such as when waiting for the subway.

    Graphics and Sound:

    The 2D graphics are simple but sufficient to create a marine environment. The sound is not overly impressive but still meets the game's requirements.
