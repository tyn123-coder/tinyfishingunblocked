Stick Merge

Help your stickman accomplish his sharpshooter missions in Stick Merge, a game that mixes shooting and idle. On the battlefield you will have to aim precisely at your enemies when they are in the open and not waste your ammunition on enemies protected behind walls or wooden boxes. Between each mission you can buy new weapons, then merge them together to increase their levels. With time and a lot of money it will be possible to unlock 50 different weapons, always more powerful according to their level. And to carry out your missions, you will need sufficient firepower that will allow you to eliminate all your enemies without dying!
