Introducing Fishing and Lines

    Embark on an innovative fusion of addictive puzzles, mesmerizing fishing experiences, and captivating visuals in Fishing and Lines! Immerse yourself in a world where balls, lines, and fishing harmoniously coexist. Your mission is to strategically cast lines and match colorful balls to catch a vast array of intriguing fish species.

Key Features

  • Colorful Ball Mania: Match balls of identical colors to create lines and capture stunning fish species, each possessing its unique appearance and point value.
  • Wide Variety of Fish: Catch an impressive selection of 140 distinct fish species, varying in both looks and scoring potential.
  • Amateur Mode: Ideal for beginners or those seeking a relaxed gaming experience, the primary objective is to score as many points as possible and amass an extensive fish collection.
  • Fishing Levels: Challenge yourself with 150 levels featuring diverse objectives and tasks, aimed at maximizing your star count and enhancing your skills.
  • Fishing Pro: For seasoned gamers, engage in the advanced Fishing Pro mode, where longer lines consisting of five or more balls of the same hue are required to ensnare elusive creatures.
  • Tournaments: Compete against fellow anglers in thrilling tournaments, vying for coveted Bronze, Silver, or Gold Cup awards.
  • Embark on this enthralling Fishing and Lines adventure and let the joy of matching colors, casting lines, and discovering exotic fish sweep you away! Happy fishing!
