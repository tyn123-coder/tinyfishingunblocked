PLAY Baldi's Basics Field Trip: Camping Remake ON TINY-FISHING

**Baldi's Basics Field Trip: Camping Remake** is a fan-made version of the original game that combines the quirky educational horror elements of **Baldi's Basics** with a camping theme. In this game, players join Baldi and his friends on a camping trip, where they encounter various challenges and tasks while trying to avoid Baldi's strict rules. The gameplay involves collecting items, solving puzzles, and navigating the environment while being mindful of Baldi's presence. With its nostalgic graphics and humor, the remake captures the essence of the original while introducing new mechanics and a fun camping twist. Fans of the series can enjoy an entertaining mix of exploration and lighthearted horror in this unique adventure!
