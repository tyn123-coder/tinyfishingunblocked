**FNAF 4** (Five Nights at Freddy's 4) takes a different approach, shifting the setting to a child's bedroom and focusing on a more psychological horror experience. Players assume the role of a young boy haunted by nightmarish versions of animatronics. The gameplay revolves around listening for sounds and using a flashlight to fend off the terrifying creatures lurking in the darkness. Unlike previous entries, **FNAF 4** emphasizes stealth and sound, requiring players to manage their resources carefully while navigating the intense fear of being caught. The game features minigames that delve into the protagonist's backstory, adding depth to the narrative. With its chilling atmosphere and innovative mechanics, **FNAF 4** pushes the boundaries of horror in the series.
